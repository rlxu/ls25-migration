-subtle-

a personal journey through interpersonal comedy


The process of transition is often continuous and made of small experiences. This is a collection of such experiences as an Asian American immigrant presented through the internet phenomenon of memes, courtesy of the wonderful subtle asian traits.

The images below focus on the conflicts of traditional Asian parenting in the Western world, composed of scenarios encountered by myself and the children of Asian American immigrants around me. There are positives, and there are negatives, but all are crucial to the formation of our personal identities.
